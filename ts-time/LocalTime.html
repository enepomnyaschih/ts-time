Default export#

Hierarchy#

  • class LocalTime

Description#

Comprises a specific time, without date and time zone relation. In other words, this is a tuple of (hour, minute, second, millisecond), or a number of milliseconds since day start (from 0 inclusive to MS_PER_DAY exclusive). You may bind time to a date with atDate method.
